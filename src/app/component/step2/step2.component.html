Create Leaderboards

The main benefit of brands partnering with Good Yellow is our unique system of Leaderboards that we have created. Leaderboards are basically categories that Members and consumers will use to find Brands. Leaderboards work to help brands with free and highly effective visibility to gain attention from potential new customers.

Leaderboards are displayed based on the highest to lowest number of sales "brand-wide" NOT a brand's sales from within that category. We also display how many different customers a brand has.

One of the most unique things about Leaderboards is that Brands are the ones who can create and title the Leaderboards. This is a chance for Partners to convey attributes about their Brand or products that they believe will attract the attention of their target audience the most. Be super creative. The title of a Leaderboard basically acts like an advertisement for Brands within that Leaderboard. Make it very specific or more broad. If the title of a Leaderboard is more specific, it will attract less Brands. The smaller number of Brands that a Leaderboard has within it, the lower it will be on a Leaderboard List and will attract much less visibility.

If there are two or more similarly titled Leaderboards, we will combine them into one single Leaderboard. Members can follow Leaderboards.

Brands

The Brands category allows you to create a Leaderboard and title it to describe something about your brand that will appeal to and connect with your target audience.

Before you do that, you must choose the primary section and the secondary section that your Leaderboard will belong to.

A leaderboard can be titled for example, a women-owned grass-fed beef jerky startup based in Denver.

Be VERY creative, the title can be broad or very specific. There are NO limits to how many Leaderboards you can create. The more Leaderboards or Leaderboard Lists you are on, the more visibility your brand will have. The higher the number of sales you have, the higher chance you have of getting new customers since you will be displayed towards the top of both Leaderboard Lists and Leaderboards.
