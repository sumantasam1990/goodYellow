Connect Your Stripe Account

We require Brands use Stripe as their payment gateway. Good Yellow will not in any way have access to your Stripe account. 100% of payments made by your customers when buying your products will go DIRECTLY into your Stripe account minus any credit card processing fees. You can then access your payments that you have received in your Stripe account.

Please start connecting your Stripe account below.

Once you have connected your Stripe account you will be able to start creating your Storefront and Product Pages.

Instructions:

✔️ Go to https://dashboard.stripe.com/apikeys

✔️ Now you can see API/Publishable key

✔️ Below Publishable key you can see Secret key. (Note: If you can not find the secret key, you can Click on the "Create Secret Key" button.)

✔️ Make sure all keys should be "Live" keys.
