How do Leaderboard Lists work?

Think of Leaderboard Lists as a way to organize commonly titled Leaderboards or even a collection of other Leaderboard Lists on one page. Leaderboard Lists will simply have the title of that Leaderboard or another Leaderboard List organized on one page by the total number of sales amongst the Brands within that Leaderboard or Leaderboard Lists. So again, the more Leaderboard Lists your Brand is on, the higher chances of continual visibility your Brand will have.
