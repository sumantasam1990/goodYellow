How will the revenue share work?

Each Partner will receive a promo code that their existing customers will have to type in when they sign up in order for them to get the discounted membership price. We will also use that promo code to know that a particular Partner signed up a customer. This way we can split the monthly or yearly membership fee with the Partner.

Every month on the last business day, we will send each Partner the revenue share for the previous 30 or 31 days via direct deposit.
